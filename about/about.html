Hi, I'm Jennifer Naasz. I'm a born adventurer form the wilds of the Upper Peninsula of Michigan. I grew up building forts in the woods and jumping off cliffs into Lake Superior. I reveled in the solitude and the magnitude of nature, absorbing anything and everything around me.

In 2013 I packed up my car and headed south, drivning 13 hours through a snowstorm in the beginning of April. I had come to Metro Detroit to start my career in health care, but managed to lose a sense of myself in the mountains of paperwork, the rat race of being understaffed and overworked, the grim outlook of the oncology ward and the smell of C-Diff.
